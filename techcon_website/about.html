History

TechCon started in the year 2000 as a small meetup for tech enthusiasts and has since grown into a global conference attracting thousands of participants each year.

Mission

Our mission at TechCon is to foster innovation and collaboration among technology professionals and enthusiasts. We strive to create an inclusive environment where ideas can be shared and developed.

Notable Past Speakers

Ajit Manocha, Former CEO of GLOBALFOUNDRIES

Ajit Manocha is the President and CEO of SEMI, the global industry association serving the semiconductor and electronics manufacturing supply chain. He previously served as the CEO of GlobalFoundries and held executive positions at Spansion, Philips/NXP Semiconductors, and AT&T Bell Laboratories. Manocha has been recognized for his contributions to the semiconductor industry, including induction into the Semiconductor Industry Hall of Fame and the Silicon Valley Engineering Hall of Fame​ (SEMI)​​ (IEEE Events)​..

Nicky Lu

Dr. Nicky Lu is the CEO and Founder of Etron Technology. He has co-founded several companies and made significant contributions to the semiconductor industry, including pioneering Taiwan's first 8-inch-wafer and advanced Logic/4MbSRAM/16MbDRAM products. Dr. Lu holds a Ph.D. from Stanford University and has received numerous awards, including an IBM Corporate Award and the IEEE Highest Technical Award in Solid-State Circuits. He is also involved in various semiconductor industry associations and initiatives​ (IEEE Events)​.
